Month
KPI Goal Actual Owner Status
✅ On Track / Hit
Pace meets or beats the monthly goal. "Hit" = goal achieved.
🟠 Behind
Trailing the goal but recoverable this month with focus.
⚠️ At Risk
Blocked or unlikely to hit without help — flag it on the Monday call.
🔴 Missed

Everything is editable — click any cell to type, pick a Status from the dropdown. All products are stacked on one page, grouped by section (each band shows that product's status mix). Use + Add a product at the bottom (or the toolbar) to add anything we're missing; rename/remove on each band. Add a new Month at the start of each month (clones owners + goals, clears actuals). Goals are set for the month (next 30 days); Matt reviews weekly; full team review mid-month (the 15th). Saves automatically in this browser; use Export CSV to share or back up.
