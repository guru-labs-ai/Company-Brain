How to read this report. The KPI bar, Sprint Breakdown, and per-person pills pull live from ClickUp every 10 minutes (Sprint 45 closed + Sprint 46 active), seeded with last-known values so nothing is ever blank. Each product card shows its near-term goal (from the Jun 15 KPI scoreboard) and an "X of Y to goal · %" — Y = the concrete milestones to reach that goal, X = how many are shipped (✓ done · ⟳ in progress · ○ next), and the % is simply X÷Y. That's real completion against the goal, anchored to milestones you can point at — not a sprint-task %. Milestones are reviewed at the monthly 15th KPI review; lifecycle status (🟢 Live / 🟡 Building / 🔵 Planned) is from Abdul's Funnel Portfolio Map.
